Who we are

Leading real estate developer & a general construction contractor.

The Cyfield Group is an integrated group of companies, and one of the key players today in all areas of the construction industry in Cyprus.

The Group has slowly and steadily evolved into one of the largest of its kind in the region, with operations in Cyprus, Greece and Egypt. In 1996 Cyfield extended its territory to Greece, and in 2013 started its first infrastructure works in the Middle East, in Egypt.
