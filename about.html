Building since 1990.

From a single contractor in Nicosia to an integrated group operating across Cyprus, Greece and Egypt — this is the Cyfield story.

Who we are

One integrated Group, one promise: we deliver what we promise.

The Cyfield Group is an integrated group of companies, and one of the key players today in all areas of the construction industry in Cyprus. The Group operates also in Greece and Egypt.

Our activities span the acquisition of land, design, construction, marketing and sale of a complete range of residential and commercial properties — as well as large-scale infrastructure and, increasingly, energy.

A steady
climb.

Milestones from a company that grows the way it builds — deliberately.

1990

The foundation

Cyfield is established in Nicosia as a general construction contractor, classified from early on among the A-Class contractors of Cyprus.

1996

Into Greece

The Group extends its real estate development and general contracting territory to Greece, opening an office in Athens.

2013

First works in Egypt

Cyfield begins its first infrastructure works in the Middle East, in Egypt — the Group's entry into large-scale international infrastructure.

2020

360 Nicosia

The tallest building in Nicosia is completed and goes on to win a European Property Award for Residential High-Rise Development.

2024

Into energy

After several photovoltaic parks, the Group begins constructing the first private thermal power plant in Cyprus — the country's largest private infrastructure project.
